RP Conduct and Tips

Author: Viowlett & Quill

Just a few things to think about when getting into our RP.

  1. General Rules

    • When Joining the RP, create a tupperbot for each of your characters.
    • One RP at a time per character. (E.g. The character can't be in two places at once)
    • Any battles/attacks will require rolls. You must be willing to accept blows to your character if you wish to involve them in combat.
    • Characters can die, but they can also be resurrected. (see 'Death & Dying')
